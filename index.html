Research

EEG classifiers in prosthetic control pipelines can be collapsed by imperceptible perturbations to the input signal.

BCIShield quantifies this across 9 subjects and two attack types, evaluates defense strategies, and identifies an unresolved gap between defended and clean-baseline accuracy — the open problem that motivates the next phase of work.

ConditionAttackAcc.Δ
Clean baseline—57.9%—
No defenseFGSM ε=0.0110.1%−47.8pp
